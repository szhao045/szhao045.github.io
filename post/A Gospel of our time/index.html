A Gospel of our time

I hate to start with “this day and age”, in this day and age, music has become very private; not just in terms of medium–where online streaming has provided access to music that is not limited by location and time, but also in content. Do forgive me in giving an cliché example: Taylor Swift’s boyfriend songs seem to represent our popular taste.

Sometimes I am perplexed by the fast speed about our time. I wonder how much of this change is remembered, by myself and by time.

History is like an under sampling of a signal

I went to listen Gospel according to the Other Mary today.

I feel I found something that can definitely be remembered by me and by time.
